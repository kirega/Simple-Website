About

Evacay Africa is Kenya's most respected and experienced tour company. We have grown to become an industry leader with enough tour vehicles to meet needs for group packages, individual packages and custom design packages. Our transport fleet consists of 7-8 seater AWD Land Cruisers and Vans for great safari experience and 28 -45 seater luxury buses fit for excursions around town and for airport transfers. Our professional tour guide drivers are expert wildlife trackers with years in experience. At Evacay Africa we work tirelessly to ensure you get the best holiday and adventurous destinations. All you have to do is pack and let us do all the work for you. Evacay Africa is determined to make your vacation magical!

Why Kenya?

Kenya is the home of African Safaris with diverse, natural and amazing sceneries, wide range traditions and culture,hand feeding giraffes,adopting orphaned elephants,breathtaking balloon safaris at dawn (Maasai Mara), white sandy beaches on the East African Coast and over 45 national parks and Reserves.Visit the Maasai mara reserve to experience the Annual Great Wild Beast Migration which is one of the wonders of the world and also enjoy Amboseli National park offering view of Africa's tallest Mountain, Mt. Kilimanjaro.
