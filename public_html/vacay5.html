Rift Valley Classic

Day 1: Nairobi to Lake Nakuru

After Safari briefing travel towards Kenyan fertile highlands to Lake Nakuru National Park arriving before mid day. Once in the lodge after lunch there is an afternoon game drive returning to the lodge as the sun sets. At the park you can enjoy the wide ecological diversity and varied habitats that range from Lake Nakuru itself to the surrounding escarpment and picturesque ridges. Lake Nakuru National Park is ideal for bird watching, hiking, picnic and game drives.

Day 2: Lake Nakuru to Lake Naivasha

Following an early game drive and breakfast proceed to Lake Naivasha for a boat ride hence proceed to the lodge in time for lunch.

Day 3: Lake Naivasha to Maasai Mara

Following an early breakfast proceed to Maasai Mara through Kenyan fertile highlands on the roads built in the World War 2 by Italians. Keeping an eye for acres of wheat and barley and getting to the Maasai Mara just in time for lunch and an afternoon game drive. Maasai Mara National Reserve is an area of preserved savannah wilderness in southwestern Kenya, along the Tanzanian border. Its animals include lions, cheetahs, elephants, zebras and hippos. Wildebeest traverse its plains during their annual migration. The landscape has grassy plains and rolling hills, and is crossed by the Mara and Talek rivers. The area nearby is dotted with villages (enkangs) of Maasai people.

Day 4: Spend day at the Maasai Mara

After an early breakfast, depart on a morning game drive, returning to the camp mid- morning. After lunch, and maybe relaxation, swim or siesta commence for an afternoon game drive.

Day 5: Maasai Mara to Nairobi

After an early breakfast, depart on a morning game drive then drive out to Nairobi and onwards transfer to the airport or a hotel for ongoing safari arrangements.
